Rafting Adventure Company

We bring courage, care, and unforgettable river journeys to every guest. Our mission is to deliver safe thrills, build community, and leave smiles on every shore. We take pride in protecting our rivers and wildlife through eco-friendly practices and education. Our mission is to create thrilling, sustainable adventures that respect the earth and inspire love for nature in everyone who joins us.

Est. 1883

History

Founded many seasons ago on the banks of a roaring river, our rafting company began with just two boats and a dream.

Over the years, we have grown from a small team of adventurous friends into a trusted name in outdoor exploration. Though the stories may sound like tall tales, our history is filled with waves, laughter, and countless unforgettable journeys shared with our clients.

At Rafting Adventure Company, we believe adventure connects people. Since our first trip down the roaring Rapids River in 2010, our mission has been to help families, thrill-seekers, and nature lovers experience the rush of whitewater in a safe, fun, and unforgettable way.

Our certified guides are passionate about rafting and the outdoors, ensuring every guest enjoys an exciting and safe adventure whether you are a beginner or a seasoned paddler. Let us make waves together!
